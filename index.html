  • 2015 - G-20 Press Conference - delivered 16 November 2015, Kaya Palazzo Resort, Antalya, Turkey

  • 2016 - Address on the 15-Year Anniversary of 9/11 - delivered 11 September 2016, Arlington, Virginia

  • 2017 - Presidential Farewell Address - delivered 10 January 2017, McCormick Place, Chicago, Illinois
